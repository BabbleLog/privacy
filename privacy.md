Privacy Policy — BabbleLog                                                             
                                                                                         
  Last updated: May 2026                                                                 
                                                                                         
  Who we are                                                                             
                                                                                         
  BabbleLog is a family memory app that helps parents capture their children's quotes,   
  milestones, and growth. It is developed independently. You can reach  
  us at babblelog.info@gmail.com.                                                         
                                                                
  ---                                                                                  
  What data BabbleLog collects
                                                                                         
  When you use BabbleLog, you may enter the following information:
                                                                                         
  - Children's names, date of birth, and gender                                          
  - Photos of your children                                                            
  - Voice recordings of things your children say                                         
  - Growth measurements (height, weight, shoe size)                                      
  - Milestone records and personal notes                                               
                                                                                         
  BabbleLog does not collect this data. All of the above is entered by you, stored on    
  your device, and optionally backed up to your personal cloud account (iCloud on iOS,   
  Google Drive on Android). We never see it, never receive it, and have no access to it. 
                                                                                       
  ---                                                                                  
  How your data is stored
                                                                                         
  On your device. All data is stored locally in the app's private storage on your phone
  using an encrypted local database (Hive). It does not leave your device unless you     
  choose to back it up.                                         
                                                                                         
  In your personal cloud. If you enable backup, your data is uploaded to your own iCloud 
  account (iOS) or your own Google Drive account (Android). This is your account —     
  governed by Apple's or Google's privacy policy — not ours. We do not have access to    
  your cloud storage.                                           
                                                                                       
  We operate no servers. BabbleLog has no backend, no user accounts, and no cloud        
  infrastructure of our own. There is no way for us to access your data.
                                                                                         
  ---                                                           
  What we do NOT do                                                                    
                   
  - We do not collect, transmit, or store your data on any server we control
  - We do not sell or share your data with third parties                                 
  - We do not display advertisements
  - We do not use analytics, crash reporting tools, or tracking SDKs                     
  - We do not require you to create an account                  
  - We do not use cookies or web tracking of any kind                                    
                                                                                         
  ---                                                                                    
  Children's privacy (COPPA)                                                             
                                                                                       
  BabbleLog is designed for use by parents, not by children. Parents enter their       
  children's information on their own behalf. We do not knowingly collect any personal   
  information directly from children under the age of 13. Because all data remains on
  your device and in your personal cloud account, parents retain full control at all     
  times.                                                        
                                                                                       
  ---
  In-App Purchases
                  
  BabbleLog offers a one-time Premium upgrade processed entirely through Apple's App
  Store. We do not collect or store any payment information. All purchase transactions   
  are handled by Apple and are subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
                                                                                         
  ---                                                           
  Your rights                                                                          
             
  Because we store no data on our servers, we have no personal data to provide, correct,
  or delete on your behalf. To delete all your BabbleLog data, simply delete the app from
   your device. To remove your backup, delete the backup file from your iCloud Drive or
  Google Drive.                                                                          
                                                                
  If you are located in the European Union, you have rights under GDPR including the     
  right to access and erasure. Since all data is under your direct control on your own
  device and cloud account, you can exercise these rights yourself at any time.          
                                                                
  ---                                                                                  
  Data security
               
  Your data is stored in the app's private sandboxed storage on your device, inaccessible
   to other apps. Cloud backups are protected by your Apple or Google account credentials
   and their respective security measures.
